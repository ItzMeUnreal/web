About unreal!

Alex Bennett, known online as unreal!, is an independent RPG developer and music producer focused on creating immersive and expressive experiences.

He is currently part of the development team behind Apocalypse of Glitches, a Roblox RPG inspired by the classic game Forsaken. The project combines experimental design, atmosphere, and storytelling to create something unique within the Roblox creative scene.

As a producer, unreal! draws influence from Toby Fox, 80s retro synth, and modern underground sounds like PluggnB, DiscoPlug, and New Jazz. His work blends nostalgic tones with futuristic rhythm, bridging emotion and creativity through both music and game design.
