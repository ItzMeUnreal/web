Apocalypse of Glitches

Apocalypse of Glitches (AOG) is an upcoming Roblox game being developed by a dedicated indie team. The game takes inspiration from Forsaken and expands on its ideas with a unique atmosphere and vision.

It focuses on storytelling, exploration, and combat in a distorted digital world filled with mysteries and strange entities. It combines mysterious environments, unique visual design, and a strong sense of atmosphere to create a distinctive RPG experience.
